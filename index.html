About

Lots of people have passions, but some simply don't have the time to pursue these dreams. Whether it be having their art be sold for a fortune, or to become a huge entrepreneur. Some people don't get to change the world around them, but we can change THEIR world.    

Our charity aims to suppor, specifically, our aspiring musicians with cancer, and we hope to treat them, and let them have fun doing so through music. We give funds for treatment and we donate free musical instruments to these patients as part of our charity.    

The instruments we donte can consist from any of the basi, well-know, instruments like guitar, bass and drums, but we also have more exotic picks like banjos, theremins, kalimbas, violin, etc. As well as providing lessons for the instruments we give.
